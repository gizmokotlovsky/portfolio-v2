graphics

This section brings together different graphic and web projects created over the years — logos, websites, business cards, printed materials and visual pieces made for different people, places and needs. The visual language changes from project to project, because every person, place and story brings its own rhythm.

audio & video

My first film projects were made around 25 years ago on a borrowed VHS camera — school documentaries about difficult post-war memory, national identity and the fragile relationships shaped by history. Later came subtitle frames in VirtualDub, school events, sailing videos and small visual experiments. Today, this old interest, enriched by a broader set of creative and technical skills, has evolved into a new form: bringing hidden texts, old ideas and unfinished moods back to life.

painting

Painting is a quiet place for me. A way to slow down emotions from the day — without explaining them too much, without making them useful, without making them perfect. Imperfect honesty.
